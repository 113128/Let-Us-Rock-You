Info

Let Us Rock you is a music festival held at Harrogate Grammar School that allows both adults and students to perfrom songs by others or ones created by them. Every year we invite a number of special guests to sing and greet their fans.

This event is the successor to the schools famous Battle of the Bands concert back in 2014.

The Event will be held at Harrogate Grammar School on the 8th August 2018 from 12:00pm to 8:00pm.
